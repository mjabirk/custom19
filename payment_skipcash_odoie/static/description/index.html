A quick guide to setting up the **Odoie Skipcash** payment module in Odoo and connecting it to your Skipcash Merchant account for immediate transaction processing.

1. Odoo Module Configuration

Configure your **Skipcash Merchant ID** and **API Key** (Test and Production) in Odoo's **Payment Providers** menu. Ensure the correct journal is linked for automatic reconciliation.

2. Skipcash Merchant Portal Setup

To receive payment status updates automatically, configure the **Webhook URL** (provided by the Odoie module) within your Skipcash Merchant Portal settings.
